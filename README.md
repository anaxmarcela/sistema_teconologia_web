# Siempre Iba a Ser El - Choose Your Own Adventure

An interactive narrative story built with HTML (Lab #2).

## About the Story

*"Siempre Iba a Ser El"* (It Was Always Going to Be Him) is a coming-of-age romance drama.

Ana has been in love with Kevin since she was twelve years old — or so she believed. When Kevin returns to their neighborhood during their final year of high school, Ana strikes an unlikely deal with her longtime nemesis and neighbor, Wesley: he will help her win Kevin's heart in exchange for the parking spot they have fought over for years.

But as the plan unfolds, Ana starts to realize that the person she thought she knew — and the feelings she thought she had — may not be what she imagined.

**Characters:**
- **Ana** — the protagonist. Stubborn, secretly romantic, and terrible at admitting she is wrong.
- **Kevin** — the boy Ana has had a crush on since childhood.
- **Wesley** — the annoying neighbor. Or so Ana always thought.

## Story Structure

- **14 HTML pages** in total: intro, 9 story pages, and 3 distinct endings
- **3 possible endings:** Good, Neutral, and Bad

### Option 1: Open directly in browser
Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.

- **HTML5 only** — No JavaScript, no CSS
- Navigation uses only the `<a>` tag
- Each page includes: headings (h1/h2), paragraphs (p), images (img), text formatting (b, strong, small, em), head, body, and footer

## Lab Requirements Fulfilled

- 14 HTML files (exceeds the minimum of 11)
- At least 2 choices per branching page
- 3 distinct and independent endings (good, neutral, bad)
- All required HTML tags present on every page
